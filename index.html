This little tool turns BitTorrent Sync Secrets into 15 words you can write down and copy more easily.

The idea and the wordlist is from the BIP 0039 reference implementation: trezor/python-mnemonic

How does it work:

btsync uses 33 characters for displaying the secret to the user. The secret only contains characters [A-Z] and [2-7].

This results in 26 + 6 = 32 = 25 different characters, which can be stored in 5 bits.

With 33 of this characters the secret has 33 Â· 5 = 165 bits of data.

With a wordlist of 2048 = 211 words the data can now be splitted into 165/11 = 15 words. And a single word then represents its number in the list as data.
